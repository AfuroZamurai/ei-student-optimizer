Exponential Idle Student Optimizer

This page will determine the most optimal distribution for your sigma / students given your current number of students, number of stars, t, and f(t).

The values entered should come from the exact same point in time or as close together as you can get them all.

For your current students, enter the number of students that have not been allocated to theories. For your t, enter the number in the top left corner. For your f(t), input the number after the ee in the top-left corner. For your current stars, go into the "Statistics" menu and enter the number under "Total Stars".

Unless you know what you're doing, you should leave the bottom four entries alone.
